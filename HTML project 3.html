Cookies

These bite-sized treats are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without overindulging. Follow this simple recipe to create delicious cookies that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C and line a baking sheet lined with baking paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda.
  3. In another bowl, beat the butter, sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture
  5. Drop small spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes, then let cool before enjoying!
